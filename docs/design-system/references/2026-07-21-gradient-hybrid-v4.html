The hybrid — G3's glow + G4's card light

One light source, told twice: the halo lights the room, the card gradients light the objects. To keep the total atmosphere at the same level as either parent, both effects run at roughly half strength in the hybrid — the halo is dimmer than G3's and the card gradient shallower than G4's. Depth should sum, not stack. Parents flank it for comparison.
